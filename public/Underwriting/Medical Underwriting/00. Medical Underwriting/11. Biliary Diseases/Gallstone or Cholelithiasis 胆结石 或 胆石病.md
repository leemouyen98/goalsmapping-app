#Medical_Underwriting/Biliary_Diseases

The presence of stones in the gallbladder.
胆囊结石是指胆囊内出现结石的状况。

Gallstones may form if bile contains too much cholesterol, too much bilirubin, or not enough bile salts. Gallstones can block the tubes (ducts) through which bile flows from your gallbladder or liver to your small intestine. Severe pain, jaundice and bile duct infection can result.
若胆汁中含有过多胆固醇、过多胆红素，或胆盐不足，则可能形成胆结石。胆结石可阻塞胆汁从胆囊或肝脏流向小肠的管道（胆管），可导致剧烈疼痛、黄疸及胆管感染。

Surgical removal of the gallbladder or gallstones is the main treatment. Around 80% of people with gallstone symptoms will need surgery. Surgeons may remove your entire gallbladder (cholecystectomy), or just the stones from bile ducts.
手术摘除胆囊或胆结石是主要治疗方式，约80%有症状的胆结石患者需要手术，外科医生可能摘除整个胆囊（胆囊切除术），或仅从胆管取出结石。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as endoscopy, blood & urine tests etc.? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如内镜、验血及尿液检查等）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？

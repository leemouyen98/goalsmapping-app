#Medical_Underwriting/Biliary_Diseases

Is a common condition caused by the storage of extra fat in the liver. Most people have no symptoms, and it doesn't cause serious problems for them. In some cases, though, it can lead to liver damage. The good news is you can often prevent or even reverse fatty liver disease with lifestyle changes.
脂肪肝是因肝脏中储存多余脂肪而引起的常见疾病，大多数人没有症状，通常不会造成严重问题；但在某些情况下，可能导致肝脏损伤。好消息是，通常可以通过改变生活方式来预防甚至逆转脂肪肝。

According to the American Liver Foundation, there are no medical treatments yet for non-alcoholic fatty liver disease. So that means eating a healthy diet and exercising regularly are the best ways to both prevent liver damage from starting or reverse liver disease once it's in the early stages.
根据美国肝脏基金会，目前尚无针对非酒精性脂肪肝的药物治疗方法，这意味着健康饮食和定期运动仍是预防肝脏损伤或在早期阶段逆转肝病的最佳途径。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as endoscopy, blood & urine tests etc.? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如内镜、验血及尿液检查等）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？

## 所需文件

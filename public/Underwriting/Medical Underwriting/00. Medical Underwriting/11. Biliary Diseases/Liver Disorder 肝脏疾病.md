#Medical_Underwriting/Biliary_Diseases

Is any disturbance of liver function that causes illness. The liver is responsible for many critical functions within the body and should it become diseased or injured, the loss of those functions can cause significant damage to the body.
肝脏疾病是指任何引起肝功能障碍并导致疾病的状况，肝脏在体内负担多项重要功能，若肝脏发病或受伤，功能丧失可对全身造成重大损害。

There are many kinds of liver diseases and conditions. Some, like hepatitis, are caused by viruses. Others can be the result of drugs or drinking too much alcohol. Long-lasting injury or scar tissue in the liver can cause cirrhosis. Jaundice, or yellowing of the skin, can be one sign of liver disease.
肝脏疾病种类繁多，部分由病毒引起（如甲型、乙型及丙型肝炎），其他可能由药物、毒素或过量饮酒引起（如脂肪肝和肝硬化）；长期损伤或肝脏瘢痕化（肝硬化）以及黄疸（皮肤变黄）均可能是肝病的征兆。

There are many kinds of liver diseases: diseases caused by viruses, such as hepatitis A, hepatitis B, and hepatitis C; diseases caused by drugs, poisons, or too much alcohol, such as fatty liver disease and cirrhosis.
肝脏疾病包括：病毒引起的疾病（如甲型、乙型及丙型肝炎）；药物、毒素或过量饮酒引起的疾病（如脂肪肝和肝硬化）。

Over time, liver disease can cause cirrhosis (scarring). As more scar tissue replaces healthy liver tissue, the liver can no longer function properly. Left untreated, liver disease can lead to liver failure and liver cancer.
随时间推移，肝脏疾病可导致肝硬化（瘢痕化），当更多瘢痕组织替代健康肝细胞时，肝脏便无法正常运作；若不及时治疗，肝病可导致肝衰竭和肝癌。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as endoscopy, blood & urine tests etc.? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如内镜、验血及尿液检查等）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？

## 所需文件

> 1) 来自客户 / 医生
> a) Any relevant reports such as liver function test, urine or scan test?
> a) 任何相关报告，如肝功能检查、尿液及扫描检查报告。

> 2) Medical attendant report (MAR) / Attending physician statement (APS)

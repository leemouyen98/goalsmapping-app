#Medical_Underwriting/Biliary_Diseases

Includes inflammation, infection, stones or blockage of the gallbladder. The gallbladder is a sac located under the liver. It stores and concentrates bile produced in the liver.
胆囊疾病包括胆囊发炎、感染、结石或阻塞。胆囊是位于肝脏下方的囊状器官，负责储存和浓缩肝脏产生的胆汁。

While gallstones are widely known, cholecystitis is actually the most common form of gallbladder disease. Cholecystitis is an inflammation of the gallbladder often caused by gallstones.
虽然胆结石广为人知，但胆囊炎实际上是最常见的胆囊疾病，胆囊炎是胆囊的炎症，通常由胆结石引起。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as endoscopy, blood & urine tests etc.? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如内镜、验血及尿液检查等）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> 1) From client / doctor
> 1) 来自客户 / 医生
> a) Any relevant reports such as liver function test, urine or scan test?
> a) 任何相关报告，如肝功能检查、尿液及扫描检查报告。
>
> 2) Medical attendant report (MAR) / Attending physician statement (APS)
> 2) 医疗随行报告（MAR）/ 主治医生声明（APS）

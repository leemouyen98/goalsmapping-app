#Medical_Underwriting/Biliary_Diseases

Acute or chronic inflammation of the gallbladder, causing severe pain, usually caused by gallstone obstruction.
胆囊炎是胆囊的急性或慢性炎症，通常由胆结石阻塞引起，导致剧烈疼痛。

Most often, cholecystitis is the result of hard particles that develop in your gallbladder (gallstones). Gallstones can block the tube (cystic duct) through which bile flows when it leaves the gallbladder.
胆囊炎最常见的原因是胆囊内形成的硬质颗粒（胆结石），胆结石可阻塞胆汁从胆囊流出的管道（胆囊管）。

The main symptom of acute cholecystitis is a sudden sharp pain in the upper right side of your abdomen that spreads towards your right shoulder. The affected part of the abdomen is usually extremely tender, and breathing deeply can make the pain worse.
急性胆囊炎的主要症状是腹部右上方突发的剧烈疼痛，并向右肩蔓延，患处腹部通常极度压痛，深呼吸会加重疼痛。

Treatment for cholecystitis usually involves a hospital stay to control the inflammation in your gallbladder. Sometimes, surgery is needed.
胆囊炎的治疗通常需要住院以控制胆囊炎症，有时需要手术治疗。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as endoscopy, blood & urine tests etc.? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如内镜、验血及尿液检查等）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？

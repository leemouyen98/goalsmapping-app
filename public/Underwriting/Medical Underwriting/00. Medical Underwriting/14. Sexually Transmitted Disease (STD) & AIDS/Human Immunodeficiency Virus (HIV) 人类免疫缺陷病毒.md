#Medical_Underwriting/Sexually_Transmitted_Disease

HIV (Human Immunodeficiency Virus) is a virus that attacks the body's immune system. If HIV is not treated, it can lead to AIDS (Acquired Immunodeficiency Syndrome).
HIV（人类免疫缺陷病毒）是一种攻击人体免疫系统的病毒。若不及时治疗，HIV 可发展为艾滋病（AIDS）。

There is currently no effective cure. Once people get HIV, they have it for life. But with proper medical care, HIV can be controlled.
目前尚无有效治愈方法。感染 HIV 后将终身携带病毒，但通过适当的医疗护理可有效控制病情。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1)  Actual & date of diagnosis ?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2)  Any investigation tests such as blood & urine tests? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查，如血液及尿液检查等？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3)  On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4)  Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？

#Medical_Underwriting/Mental_Diseases

Refers to a collection of symptoms that affect the mind, where there has been some loss of contact with reality. During an episode of psychosis, a person's thoughts and perceptions are disrupted and they may have difficulty recognizing what is real and what is not.
精神病是指一组影响精神的症状，患者与现实的联系已出现部分断裂。在精神病发作期间，患者的思维和感知受到干扰，可能难以分辨什么是真实的，什么不是。

Psychosis can also be triggered by traumatic experiences, stress, or physical conditions, such as Parkinson's disease, a brain tumour, or as a result of drug misuse or alcohol misuse.
精神病也可能由创伤经历、压力或身体疾病（如帕金森病、脑肿瘤）引发，或源于药物滥用或酗酒。

Psychosis can be treated, and many people make a good recovery, especially if they get help early.
精神病是可以治疗的，许多患者可以康复良好，尤其是及早寻求帮助者。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as scanning, blood & urine tests etc.? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如扫描、验血及尿液检查等）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？

## 所需文件

> 1) 来自客户 / 医生

> a) 任何相关报告，如验血、尿液及扫描检查报告。

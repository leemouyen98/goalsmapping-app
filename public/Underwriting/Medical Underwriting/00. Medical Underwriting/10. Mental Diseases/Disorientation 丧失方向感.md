#Medical_Underwriting/Mental_Diseases

The state produced by loss of awareness of space, time or personality. Occurs when you are confused about the time, where you are or even who you are.
定向障碍是丧失对空间、时间或自我认知的状态，患者会对时间、身处地点甚至自身身份感到困惑。

It can be the result of drugs, anxiety or organic disease.
定向障碍可由药物、焦虑或器质性疾病引起。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as scanning, blood & urine tests etc.? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如扫描、验血及尿液检查等）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> 1) From client / doctor
> 1) 来自客户 / 医生
> a) Any relevant reports such as blood, urine or scan test?
> a) 任何相关报告，如验血、尿液及扫描检查报告。
>
> 2) Medical Examination / Medical attendant report (MAR) / Attending physician statement (APS)
> 2) 体格检查 / 医疗随行报告（MAR）/ 主治医生声明（APS）

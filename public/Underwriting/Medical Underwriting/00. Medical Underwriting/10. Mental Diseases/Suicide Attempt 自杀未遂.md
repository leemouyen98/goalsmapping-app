#Medical_Underwriting/Mental_Diseases

A suicide attempt is an attempt to die by suicide that results in survival.
自杀未遂是指试图以自杀方式死亡但最终存活的行为。

People who attempt either hanging or carbon monoxide poisoning and survive can face permanent brain damage. Individuals who take a drug overdose and survive can face severe organ damage.
曾尝试上吊或一氧化碳中毒并存活者，可能面临永久性脑损伤；药物过量服用并存活者，可能面临严重的器官损伤。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as scanning, blood & urine tests etc.? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如扫描、验血及尿液检查等）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> 1) From client / doctor
> 1) 来自客户 / 医生
> a) Any relevant reports such as blood, urine or scan test?
> a) 任何相关报告，如验血、尿液及扫描检查报告。
> b) Mental Health Questionnaire by applicant
> b) 申请人填写心理健康问卷
>
> 2) KIV – Medical Examination / Medical attendant report (MAR) / Attending physician statement (APS)
> 2) 备查 – 体格检查 / 医疗随行报告（MAR）/ 主治医生声明（APS）

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> Suicide attempt due to depression on dd/mm/yy, seen by Dr xxx of yy Hospital. Admitted for 3 days for observation and stabilization.
> 于 dd/mm/yy 因抑郁症自杀未遂，由 yy 医院 Dr xxx 医生诊治，住院3天接受观察及病情稳定治疗。
> Currently on medication & psychiatric follow up regularly.
> 目前在定期服药及接受精神科随访。

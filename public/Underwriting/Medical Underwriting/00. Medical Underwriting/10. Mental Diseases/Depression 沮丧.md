#Medical_Underwriting/Mental_Diseases

Is a common and serious medical illness that negatively affects how you feel, the way you think and how you act. It causes a persistent feeling of sadness and loss of interest.
抑郁症是一种常见且严重的精神疾病，会对感受、思维及行为方式产生负面影响，主要表现为持续的悲伤感和对事物失去兴趣。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as scanning, blood & urine tests etc.? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如扫描、验血及尿液检查等）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> 1) From client / doctor
> 1) 来自客户 / 医生
> a) Any relevant reports such as blood, urine or scan test?
> a) 任何相关报告，如验血、尿液及扫描检查报告。
> b) Mental Health Questionnaire by applicant
> b) 申请人填写心理健康问卷
>
> 2) KIV – Medical Examination / Medical attendant report (MAR) / Attending physician statement (APS)
> 2) 备查 – 体格检查 / 医疗随行报告（MAR）/ 主治医生声明（APS）

> 目前在定期服药及随访。

## UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES
## 核保结果

> Life:
> 人寿保险：
> – Decline / Postpone / Sub-standard case depending on the severity of diagnosis
> – 根据诊断严重程度，可拒保、延迟承保或次标准承保
>
> TPD / CI / Medical insurance:
> 全残保险 / 严重疾病保险 / 医疗保险：
> – Declined case
> – 拒保
>
> ** Can be considered standard case if ceased medical follow up for more than 5 years
> ** 若停止医疗随访超过5年，可考虑标准承保

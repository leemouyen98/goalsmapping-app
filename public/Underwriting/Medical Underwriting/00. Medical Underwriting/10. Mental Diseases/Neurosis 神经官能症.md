#Medical_Underwriting/Mental_Diseases

Any long-term mental or behavioural disorder in which contact with reality is retained and the condition is recognized by the sufferer as abnormal, involving symptoms of stress (depression, anxiety, obsessive behaviour, hypochondria).
神经官能症是任何长期的精神或行为障碍，患者能保持与现实的接触，且自身意识到这种状况的异常，症状涉及压力（包括抑郁、焦虑、强迫行为、疑病症等）。

Irritants include chronic stress at work, disputes at home, household troubles, health problems, loss of loved ones, financial issues, and so on. Overwork, insufficient rest, and an inability to relax all contribute to the development of neurosis.
诱发因素包括长期工作压力、家庭纠纷、家庭困难、健康问题、失去亲人及经济问题等；过度工作、休息不足及无法放松，均会促进神经官能症的发展。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as scanning, blood & urine tests etc.? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如扫描、验血及尿液检查等）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？

#Medical_Underwriting/Digestive_Diseases

Dehydration occurs when the body loses more fluid than it takes in, upsetting the balance of minerals — salts and sugars — which affects normal bodily functions. Water makes up over two-thirds of the healthy human body.
脱水是指身体失去的液体多于摄入量，导致体内矿物质（盐分与糖分）失衡，影响正常生理功能。水分占健康人体重量的三分之二以上。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as endoscopy, blood & urine tests, etc.? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如内镜、血液及尿液检查等）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly, etc.)?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> 1) From client / doctor
> 1) 来自客户 / 医生
> a) Any relevant reports such as blood / urine / scan test?
> a) 任何相关报告，如血液/尿液/扫描检查？
>
> 2) KIV Medical attendant report (MAR) / Attending physician statement (APS)
> 2) 备查 – 医疗随行报告（MAR）/ 主治医生声明（APS）

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> Noticed dehydration due to diarrhoea on dd/mm/yy. Seen by Dr xxx of yy hospital. No abnormality found — advised to drink more water.
> dd/mm/yy 因腹泻引起脱水，由 yy 医院 Dr xxx 医生诊治，无异常发现，建议多喝水。
> Fully recovered since.
> 自此已完全康复。

A hernia occurs when abdominal contents protrude through a gap or weakness in the muscular wall. The most common hernias occur in the belly or groin area. They are caused by a combination of internal pressure and muscle weakness — which may be present at birth or develop later in life.
疝气是指腹腔内容物通过肌肉壁的缺口或薄弱处向外突出。最常见于腹部或腹股沟区域，由内部压力与肌肉薄弱共同作用引起，可先天存在或后天形成。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as scanning, blood & urine tests, etc.? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如扫描、血液及尿液检查等）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly, etc.)?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？

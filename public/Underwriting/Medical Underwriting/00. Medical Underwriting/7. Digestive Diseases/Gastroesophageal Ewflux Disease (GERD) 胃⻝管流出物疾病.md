#Medical_Underwriting/Digestive_Diseases

Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease (GERD) occurs when stomach acid repeatedly flows back into the oesophagus, irritating its lining. Risk factors include being overweight, overeating, and consuming caffeine, alcohol, chocolate or spicy foods. Untreated, GERD can cause oesophageal swelling or even oesophageal cancer.
胃食道反流病（GERD）是指胃酸反复逆流至食道，刺激食道内壁的疾病。危险因素包括超重、过量进食及摄入咖啡因、酒精、巧克力或辛辣食物。若不治疗，可能导致食道炎症甚至食道癌。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as endoscopy (OGDS), blood & urine tests, etc.? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如胃镜（OGDS）、血液及尿液检查等）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly, etc.)?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？

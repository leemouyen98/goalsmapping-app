#Medical_Underwriting/Digestive_Diseases

Diarrhoea is the frequent passing of loose, watery and unformed stools. Acute diarrhoea involves three or more loose stools per day lasting less than 14 days. The most common cause is intestinal infection, such as gastroenteritis or food poisoning.
腹泻是指频繁排出稀薄、水状或不成形的大便。急性腹泻指每日排便三次或以上，持续不超过14天，最常见原因为肠道感染，如肠胃炎或食物中毒。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as endoscopy, blood & urine tests, etc.? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如内镜、血液及尿液检查等）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly, etc.)?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？

## 所需文件

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> Noticed diarrhoea on dd/mm/yy due to food poisoning. Seen by Dr xxx of yy hospital. No abnormality found.

> 自此已完全康复。

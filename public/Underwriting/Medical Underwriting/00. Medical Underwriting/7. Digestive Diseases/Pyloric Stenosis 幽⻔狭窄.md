#Medical_Underwriting/Digestive_Diseases

Pyloric stenosis is the narrowing of the pylorus — the muscular outlet of the stomach — which delays the passage of stomach contents into the duodenum. The exact cause is unknown, though genetic and environmental factors may play a role.
幽门狭窄是胃出口（幽门）肌肉收窄，导致胃内容物进入十二指肠的通道受阻的疾病。确切病因尚不明确，遗传及环境因素可能共同发挥作用。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as endoscopy (OGDS), blood & urine tests, etc.? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如胃镜（OGDS）、血液及尿液检查等）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly, etc.)?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> 1) From client / doctor
> 1) 来自客户 / 医生
> a) Any relevant reports such as blood / urine / chest X-ray, scanning tests?
> a) 任何相关报告，如血液/尿液/胸部X光及扫描检查？
> b) Surgery report if performed.
> b) 如有进行，附上手术报告。

> 2) Medical attendant report (MAR) / Attending physician statement (APS)
> 2) 医疗随行报告（MAR）/ 主治医生声明（APS）

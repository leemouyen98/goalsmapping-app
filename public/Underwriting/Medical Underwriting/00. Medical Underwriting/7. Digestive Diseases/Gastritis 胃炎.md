#Medical_Underwriting/Digestive_Diseases

Gastritis is inflammation and irritation of the stomach lining. Common triggers include excessive alcohol consumption, spicy foods, and prolonged use of non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs).
胃炎是胃黏膜发炎及受刺激的状况，常见诱因包括过量饮酒、辛辣食物及长期使用非甾体抗炎药（NSAIDs）。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as endoscopy (OGDS), blood & urine tests, etc.? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如胃镜（OGDS）、血液及尿液检查等）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly, etc.)?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？

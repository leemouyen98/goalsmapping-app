#Medical_Underwriting/Sexually_Transmitted_Disease

Genital herpes is a sexually transmitted disease (STD) caused by the herpes simplex virus (HSV). It can cause sores on the genital or rectal area, buttocks, and thighs, and is spread through vaginal, anal, or oral sex with an infected person.
生殖器疱疹是由单纯疱疹病毒（HSV）引起的性传播疾病。可在生殖器、直肠区域、臀部及大腿内侧出现疮口，通过与感染者进行阴道、肛交或口交而传播。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1)  Actual & date of diagnosis ?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2)  Any investigation tests such as blood & urine tests? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查，如血液及尿液检查等？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3)  On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4)  Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？

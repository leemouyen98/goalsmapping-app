#Medical_Underwriting/Sexually_Transmitted_Disease

AIDS (Acquired Immunodeficiency Syndrome) is a chronic, life-threatening condition caused by the human immunodeficiency virus (HIV). HIV damages the immune system by destroying CD4 cells, making the body unable to fight infections and disease. HIV infection takes years to develop into full-blown AIDS.
艾滋病（获得性免疫缺陷综合征）是由人类免疫缺陷病毒（HIV）引起的慢性、危及生命的疾病。HIV 通过破坏 CD4 细胞损害免疫系统，使身体无法抵抗感染和疾病。HIV 感染需数年时间才会发展为完全性艾滋病。

> 2) 是否进行过任何检查，如血液及尿液检查等？如是，请说明日期及结果。

#Medical_Underwriting/Skeleton_Diseases

A bone fracture is a break in the continuity of a bone. Most fractures occur due to high-force impact or stress. However, fractures can also result from medical conditions that weaken bones, such as osteoporosis or certain types of cancer.
骨折是骨骼连续性中断的损伤，大多数骨折由高冲击力或强压力引起。骨折也可因骨质疏松症或某些癌症等削弱骨骼的疾病而发生。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as CT / MRI scan, X-ray, blood & urine tests, etc.? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如CT/MRI扫描、X光、血液及尿液检查等）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly, etc.)?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> Diagnosed with left humerus fracture due to an accident 12 years ago. Consulted Dr xxx of yy hospital — surgery with internal fixation performed. Removal of fixation not required as advised.
> 12年前因意外导致左肱骨骨折，已咨询 yy 医院 Dr xxx 医生并进行内固定手术，据悉无需取出固定物。
> Fully recovered with no limping or gait abnormality.
> 完全康复，无跛行或步态异常。

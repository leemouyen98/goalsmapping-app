#Medical_Underwriting/Skeleton_Diseases

Rheumatism is an umbrella term covering arthritis and several other conditions affecting the joints, tendons, muscles, ligaments and bones. Attacks may last a few hours or several days and tend to follow a similar pattern each time. Palindromic rheumatism typically does not affect parts of the body outside the joints and tendons.
风湿病是关节炎及其他影响关节、肌腱、肌肉、韧带和骨骼疾病的统称。发作可持续数小时至数天，通常每次症状相似。回文性风湿症通常不影响关节和肌腱以外的部位。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as CT / MRI scan, X-ray, blood & urine tests, etc.? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如CT/MRI扫描、X光、血液及尿液检查等）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly, etc.)?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？

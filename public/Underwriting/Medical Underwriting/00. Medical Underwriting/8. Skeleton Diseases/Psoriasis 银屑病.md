#Medical_Underwriting/Skeleton_Diseases

Psoriasis is a chronic skin disease that causes itchy, scaly rashes, most commonly on the knees, elbows, trunk and scalp. There is no cure. Many people with psoriasis also have serious conditions such as diabetes, heart disease or depression. Some develop psoriatic arthritis.
银屑病（牛皮癣）是一种慢性皮肤病，在膝盖、肘部、躯干及头皮等部位出现瘙痒、有鳞屑的皮疹，目前无根治方法。许多患者同时患有糖尿病、心脏病或抑郁症等严重疾病，部分患者还会发展为银屑病性关节炎。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as X-ray, blood & urine tests, etc.? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如X光、血液及尿液检查等）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly, etc.)?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？

#Medical_Underwriting/Skeleton_Diseases

Atrophy is the decrease in the size of a tissue or organ due to cellular shrinkage, caused by malnutrition, aging, genetics, physical inactivity or certain medical conditions. An exercise programme — including pool-based exercises to reduce muscle workload and other forms of rehabilitation — may help treat muscle atrophy.
萎缩症是指组织或器官因细胞萎缩而体积缩小的状况，可由营养不良、老化、遗传、缺乏运动或某些疾病引起。运动计划（包括在游泳池中进行的减轻肌肉负荷的运动及其他康复训练）可能有助于改善肌肉萎缩。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as CT / MRI scan, X-ray, blood & urine tests, etc.? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如CT/MRI扫描、X光、血液及尿液检查等）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly, etc.)?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？

#Medical_Underwriting/Skeleton_Diseases

Osteomyelitis is inflammation or swelling of the bone, resulting from an infection that has spread from elsewhere in the body or started directly in the bone — often after an injury. It is a serious condition requiring prompt treatment, usually with antibiotics. Complete the full course of medication even if symptoms improve early.
骨髓炎是骨骼发炎或肿胀的疾病，可由其他部位感染蔓延至骨骼，或直接在骨骼中发生（常见于受伤后）。骨髓炎是需要及时治疗的严重疾病，通常以抗生素治疗，即使症状改善也须完成全部疗程。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as CT / MRI scan, X-ray, blood & urine tests, etc.? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如CT/MRI扫描、X光、血液及尿液检查等）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly, etc.)?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？
